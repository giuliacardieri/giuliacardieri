WHY MAKE GAME FOR A SECRET SANTA GIFT?

In 2013/2014 I had a great opportunity to be an exchange student at Rochester Institute Of Technology with other Brazilian students. We became friends but Brazil is a huge country and most of us don't live near each other. Last christmas we decided to make a secret santa sending virtual gifts. Some of my friends sang, other made funny videos but I decided to make a game with references to almost everyone who shared this amazing experience!

PLAY MARINA AND THE LOST STUFF

SHOULD I PLAY IF I DON'T KNOW THE REFERENCES?

The game is more fun if you know everyone included and how strong the wind at RIT is. But you can play it and try to guess which items belong to Marina!
